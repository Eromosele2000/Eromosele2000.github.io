What makes us Green?

The Green Group's investment porfolio is diversified across multiple asset classes and geographies with majority and minority nivestment in listed companies, private companies, venture capitaland real estate in both Green branded and non branded companies. The Green Group is made up of the Green holdings and its several subsidiaries.

At Green, we create unique customer experiences, challenge the statu quo and champion people and the planet. Our purpose is to change business for good, and to serve our customers in the best possible way.
